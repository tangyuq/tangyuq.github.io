I am an Applied Scientist at Microsoft. I am currently a member of the Microsoft Research CNTK team. My current work is on the development of the CNTK deep learning tool. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow and then a Project Scientist at the Robotics Institute of Carnegie Mellon University.

I am interested in the establishment of social intelligence for a system of agents (pure machine agents or hybrid human-machine agents). In particular, my research focuses on two sub-topics:

  • interaction and communication mechanisms for intelligent agents, and
  • multiagent planning using symbolic logics, model checking, stochastic processes, and machine learning approaches.
